## Problem Statement

Sales teams are drowning in fragmented tools. CRM systems from Salesforce, HubSpot, and Zoho are:
- **Expensive** — $150+/user/month for enterprise features
- **Slow** — Centralized servers mean 200-500ms latency globally
- **Complex** — 6-month implementation, weeks of training
- **Stuck in the past** — Monolithic architectures that can't leverage AI natively

Meanwhile, AI has transformed every industry except sales operations. Teams still:
- Manually score leads (no data-driven prioritization)
- Guess at pipeline health (no real-time insights)
- Spend hours on data entry (no automation)
- React to problems (no predictive intelligence)

**The result:** 70% of sales time wasted on non-selling activities, 60% of leads never contacted, and billions in lost revenue.

## Solution: EdgeForce CRM

EdgeForce is the **first AI-powered CRM built entirely on the edge** — delivering Salesforce-beating performance at a fraction of the cost.

### What We Do

- **AI-Powered Lead Scoring** — Workers AI analyzes every contact in real-time, predicting conversion probability with 90%+ accuracy
- **Sub-50ms Response Times** — Cloudflare's global edge network means instant responsiveness everywhere
- **Zero-Config Setup** — Connect your first lead in 60 seconds, not 60 days
- **Complete Automation** — Email sequences, SMS campaigns, task creation, workflow triggers — all AI-driven

### How We Do It

We built the entire CRM stack on Cloudflare's edge:
- **Workers AI** for real-time inference and lead scoring
- **D1 Database** for SQLite at the edge (27 tables, millisecond queries)
- **Durable Objects** for real-time collaboration
- **R2 Storage** for files and media
- **KV** for sessions and caching

### Who It's For

- **SaaS startups** needing enterprise CRM without enterprise cost
- **Sales agencies** managing multiple client pipelines
- **Small businesses** wanting AI without data science teams
- **Enterprise** escaping legacy CRM lock-in
